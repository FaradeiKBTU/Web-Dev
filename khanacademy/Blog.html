Second post

Yesterday I decided to cook borscht by myself.
It was my first time cooking this dish, and I was a little nervous.

First, I prepared all the ingredients: meat, potatoes, beetroot, carrots, onion, and cabbage. I cut the vegetables and started cooking the soup step by step.
The process took a lot of time, but it was interesting.

At first, I thought I would make mistakes, but everything went better than I expected.
The borscht had a nice color and a good smell.
When I tried it, I was surprised — it was really tasty.

In the end, I was proud of myself.
Now I know that I can cook borscht, and I want to try cooking it again in the future.
